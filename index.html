Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we protect your information and what we do with it.

What information do we collect?

We do not collect any personal information from our users. Our app, Brainiac Battles, is designed to be fun and engaging without the need to collect any personal data.

Advertisements

We use AppLovin for displaying advertisements within our app. We do not collect or share any personal information with AppLovin, but they may collect some data for ad targeting purposes. For more information about their privacy practices, please visit AppLovin's Privacy Policy.

Open Trivia Database

Our app uses the Open Trivia Database to provide trivia questions. Open Trivia Database does not collect any personal data from our users. For more information about their privacy practices, please visit Open Trivia Database's website.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about our privacy practices.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us.
